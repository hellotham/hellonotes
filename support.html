Getting started

  • Launch HelloNotes and click Select Vault Folder.
  • Choose any folder of Markdown files — or an empty folder to start fresh. A new note is created with New Note (or ⌘N).
  • Write in Markdown. Type [[ to link a note, # to add a tag, and $…$ for math.
  • Open the Graph View to see how your notes connect, and Ask Vault to get on-device answers grounded in your notes.

Frequently asked questions

Where are my notes stored?

As plain .md files in the folder you selected. HelloNotes never moves them elsewhere; you can open them in any other app.

Do I need an account or the internet?

No. HelloNotes works entirely offline and has no account. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Why don’t I see the “Intelligence” features?

Summarise, Suggest and “Ask your vault” require a Mac with Apple Intelligence enabled (Apple silicon, a recent macOS, and Apple Intelligence turned on in System Settings). Everything else works without it.

Can I sync between devices?

Put your vault folder in any file-syncing location (iCloud Drive, Dropbox, etc.), or use the built-in Git features with your own remote. Your files, your choice.

What are the system requirements?

macOS 15 or later.
