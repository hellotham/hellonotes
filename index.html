Everything a knowledge base should be

Powerful where it counts, and out of your way everywhere else.

📄

Local-first, plain files

Your notes are ordinary .md files in a folder you choose. No lock-in, no database, readable in any editor.

✍️

Live Markdown

A native editor with live styling, syntax-highlighted code, inline LaTeX math, Mermaid diagrams, callouts and hidden comments.

🔗

Connected thinking

[[Wiki-links]] and heading links with autocomplete, backlinks, unlinked mentions, note transclusion, and an interactive graph view.

🔎

Find anything

Full-text search, “Open Quickly”, nested #tags with autocomplete, bookmarks, daily notes and templates.

✨

On-device intelligence

Summarise, suggest tags and links, expand a stub, or “ask your vault” — powered by Apple Intelligence, entirely on your Mac.

🕘

Version history with Git

Built-in Git: initialise a repo, browse a note’s history and restore earlier versions. Export to HTML or PDF anytime.
